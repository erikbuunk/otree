For each of the 10 questions, you can decide whether you want the computer to remove 3 incorrect answers, by clicking the "Advice"-button on the screen. When you see the two remaining answer options, you can then choose the one you consider correct now. One of the two remaining answer options is correct. The answer you have chosen in Part I is highlighted.

The screenshots below show for an example question how the screen looks (1) on which you make your decision to seek advice or not for any answer you just gave in part 1, and, (2) subsequently, when you decided to seek advice, i.e., three incorrect answer options are removed.

If you decide to seek advice, you will have {{Constants.timeout_advice}} seconds to submit your new answer. If you timed out with no new answer submitted, your initial answer form Part 1 will count for your payment.

If you do not want to seek advice on a question, you can proceed by clicking the “Continue”-Button instead. In this case, you cannot revise your answer to that question.
