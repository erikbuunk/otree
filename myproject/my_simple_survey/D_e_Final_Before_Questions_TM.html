You now have a chance to seek advice and revise answers to the previous questions. If you seek advice on a question, the computer removes 3 incorrect answers. You can then change the answer that you have submitted in Part 1 to the answer that you consider correct now.

You pay a fee of {{Constants.advice_cost}} GBP if you seek advice on a question.

With your decision to seek advice, you can also influence the chance that the computer selects the high piece rate ({{Constants.high_pr}}) or the low piece rate ({{Constants.low_pr}}) for rewarding correct answers:

  • If you pass on the option to seek advice for all 10 questions, there is an 80% chance that the computer selects the high piece rate and a 20% chance that the computer selects the low piece rate.
  • If you seek advice on at least one question, this chance is lowered to 50%, making it equally likely that the computer selects the high or the low piece rate for correct answers.
